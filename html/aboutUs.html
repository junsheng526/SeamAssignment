Origin of Moonstreet Restaurant

The inspiration for Moon Street comes from the five partners of the restaurant, who walk in the evening and feel relaxed when walking with the moon. This is the reason that we restaurants call this name. We hope that when our customers come into our restaurant, they will feel a sense of relaxation and chilling.


Embracing the sublime pleasure of drink, the joy of good food and the comfort of family, Moonstreet is the place that suitable sharing the simple indulgences of life with friends.
